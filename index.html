How It Works

From finished edit to
delivered film in 5 minutes.

No technical setup. No re-uploading. Works with the YouTube and Vimeo links you already have.

01
🎬
Create a Gallery
Enter your client's name, event date, and choose a template. Quince or wedding — chapters are pre-labeled and ready. Takes 60 seconds.
02
🔗
Paste Your Video Links
Drop in your YouTube or Vimeo links for each chapter. No re-uploading, no compression, no waiting. Your videos are already online — we just make them beautiful.
03
✉️
Send the Link
CineVia sends a branded email and SMS to your client with their private access code. They click, enter the code, and their film opens like a studio delivery.
